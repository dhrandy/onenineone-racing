DRIVER INFORMATION

  • Driver: Frank Peters
  • From: Tower City, PA
  • Hometown: Concord, NC
  • First Race: 1996
  • Day Job: Design Engineer

Frank started racing in 1996 in the Quarter Midgets Class at Blue Mountain all originating from the families’ love and dedication for racing. His first win was that same year including the 1996 National Champion in Quarter Midgets Beginners Class. After excelling in quarter midgets for several podium finishes and wins. In 2005 the family felt it was time to get back into the open wheel racing by purchasing a 600cc micro sprint. Proving to be successful in winged cars, in 2017 chose to purchase a 305 sprint car and never looked back. The first short year in 2018 is becoming a great learning experience for both driver and crew, quickly getting up to speed on what is needed to become a successful and respected team.
